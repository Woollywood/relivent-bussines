Our ticket sales platform emphasizes protecting your personal information and financial transactions. Your data is securely housed within our systems using state-of-the-art encryption methods. Furthermore, we ensure that your payment card information is kept confidential and that no unauthorized transactions occur. Trust us, your data isn't going anywhere, and neither is your money.
Relivent prioritizes data protection using advanced encryption methods such as AES, RSA, and SHA-3. AES secure data during data transfer , RSA assists in secure key exchange, while SHA-3 ensures data integrity. We also use tokenization for credit/debit cards security and comply with PCI DSS standards. Your data and money are well secured with us.
A convenient list of all your orders that displays all necessary information about the purchase, advanced features of personal account allows you to analyze your orders within the account. You can compare indicators, track trends and generate reports.
Collect statistics data directly in your personal account. As well you can download it, edit and send. We generate reports on purchases, returns, visits and constantly expand the range of data that will help you improve your approach to event promotion and marketing.
Download and edit financial reports using our tools inside your personal account. You have full access to all parameters and you can track changes in real time, as well as you can assign limited rights to your employees to analyze statistics when you don't have enough time. You can receive a report by mail every day to manage your business online.

Easy to start

Our team takes care of each client, you can always get advice and solve the necessary problem.

01 Complete the Form

Begin the process by providing basic information about yourself via a brief form. This will enable us to create a personalized offer that meets your unique needs.

02 Meet your personal manаger

We are assigning you personal manager which will contact you with prepared specially for you offer. The assigned personal manager is always available for you and happy to help you with all your questions.

03 Agreement Formalities

The next step involves formalizing our partnership through a service agreement. This contract can be signed remotely or at our head office in Warsaw, based on your convenience.

04 Access Details and User Guide

We will set up your exclusive organizer account in our Relivent system following the contract. You will receive all necessary login details, along with a detailed guide on how to use the system. This guide will enable you to independently manage events, review sales statistics, and analyze various metrics.

05 Event Creation

With your account ready, we will assist you in adding your first event to our platform. Your personal manager will provide constant support during this process, helping with any questions or issues that may arise.

06 Start Selling

Now you're all set to begin the ticket sales process. Our system ensures a smooth ticketing experience for you and your clients while providing comprehensive analytics to keep track of your performance. Enjoy the convenience of selling tickets and watching your events succeed.
